    use of alt in image

    Alt attributes enable screen readers to read the information about on-page images for the benefit of a person with complete lack of sight, visually impaired, or who is otherwise unable to view the images on the page.

    use of title tag in image

    The title attribute (or universal attribute), shows the user information about the image after a short delay, when the cursor is brought over the image (mouse-over), not to be confused with the meta title, which describes the content of the entire page, and is relevant for the snippet. The title attribute is primarily for usability purposes, and is particularly important when no image signature is available. In this case, for example, the copyright can also be inserted into the title attributes.
